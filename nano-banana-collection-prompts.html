Understanding AI Generation Parameters

CFG Scale

Controls how strictly the AI follows your prompt. Higher values (10-12) ensure precision, while lower values (8-9) allow for more creative freedom.

Steps

The number of iterations for generation. More steps (40-50) generally yield higher quality but take more time. A good range is 28-50.

Success Rate

An estimate of how often this prompt produces great results. Rates above 90% are excellent, while 80-90% still provide solid outcomes.

Pro Tip: For most nano banana figures, we recommend starting with CFG=9 and Steps=36. This combination balances quality and speed with a ~92% success rate for optimal results.
